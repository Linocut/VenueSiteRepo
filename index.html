Out of Character Rules

1. Be respectful to staff and other guests.

2. No harassing neighboring homes or venues.

3. Mature conversations allowed, but no erotic roleplaying.

Roleplaying Rules

1. Disputes may result in trial by combat with staff in Wolves Den.

2. Triple Triad matches must have a wager on them, preferably krill.

3. Anyone can play or dance on stage, at any time.

4. If you dine and dash, Jill will kick your ass when you come back.

5. You may sit outside on the property or to the shore, just let staff know.

6. Dry yourself off after going to the beach to prevent slips.

7. Don't put too much stress on the support beam.

Staff Roleplaying preferences

You may roleplay however you like. Staff has personal preferences such as using "quotes" to signify speaking and /em to make an emotion. Emotes are often used to show exaggerated or fun emotions, same with taking out or putting away weapons.
